Lasagna

Description

The best lasagna in the world!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 5 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1 pint Part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
    1. Cook lasagna noodles in a large pot until al dente.
    2. Drain lasagna noodles and rinse with cool water.
  2. Cook the meat sauce
    1. Cook ground beef in large sauce pan.
    2. Drain excess grease from the cooked ground beef.
    3. Combine tomato sauce and tomato paste.
    4. Add the combined sauce and paste into the sauce pan with the ground beef.
    5. Simmer until bubbly.
    6. Add garlic salt and Italian seasoning.
    7. Reduce heat to low simmer.
    8. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Assemble the layers
    1. Spread a thin layer of sauce at the bottom of a 13x9 inch baking dish.
    2. Add a layer of noodles.
    3. Add a layer of ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
    4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until the dish is filled.
    5. Garnish the top layer with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake
    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C.
    2. Bake for 30 minutes or until bubbly.
    3. Let it stand for 15 minutes after taking it out of the oven.
    4. Serve and enjoy.
